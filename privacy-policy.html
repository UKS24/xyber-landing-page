Xyber Privacy Policy

At Xyber, your privacy and security are our top priorities. This policy outlines how we handle your data and how our app helps protect you from common cyber threats.

1. Data Access and Collection:

  • Xyber does not collect or store your phone number, message content, or any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • We access your messages only to scan for links within them. This ensures we can protect you in real-time from malicious or unsafe links.
  • No message contents are stored or transmitted outside of your device.

2. How We Protect You:

Threat actors/attackers and scammers attempt to target you using four primary methods:

  1. SMS: Attackers may send phishing or malicious links in SMS messages that attempt to steal your personal information or install harmful software on your device.
  2. Phone Calls: Scammers may use phone calls to trick you into providing sensitive information or direct you to malicious websites or applications.
  3. Emails: Emails are often used to deliver phishing links, malicious attachments, or social engineering attacks aimed at stealing your passwords, financial details, or infecting your device.
  4. Files: Attackers may send files with embedded payloads or unsafe code that could harm your device or steal your data once opened.
